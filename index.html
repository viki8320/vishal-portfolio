Key Insights

What 2 Years Taught Me

01

Packaging = Conversion

Showing the product in real-use context in creatives massively improved CTR and conversions. Visual proof sells packaging better than any copy.

02

System > Hacks

One-off tactics give brief spikes. Building compounding systems — SEO, automation, retargeting — creates durable growth that doesn't disappear when you pause.

03

SEO + Ads Combo Wins

Organic builds trust and reduces CAC over time. Paid ads bridge the gap while SEO compounds. Running both creates a competitive moat.
